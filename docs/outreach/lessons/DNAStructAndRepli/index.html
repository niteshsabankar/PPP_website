DNA Structure and Replication

Abstract

  1. Effect of DNA replication on transmission and/or conservation of genetic information
  2. Functional relationships between DNA, genes, alleles, and chromosomes and their roles in inheritance
  3. Understanding how DNA carries the instructions for specifying the characteristics of each organism
  4. Understanding that DNA is a large polymer formed from four subunits: A, G, C, and T
  5. Chemical and structural properties of DNA
